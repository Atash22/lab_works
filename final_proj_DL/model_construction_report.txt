------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. KEY OBSERVATIONS & ANALYSIS
------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Steady Learning Curve: The model demonstrated clear and steady learning 
  across 20 epochs. The validation bounding box loss (val/box_loss) 
  decreased from initial epochs to 1.698, and classification loss 
  (val/cls_loss) dropped to 1.655.
* Progress Trend: The mAP50 metric climbed consistently from early epochs, 
  surpassing 51% by epoch 20, indicating that additional training epochs 
  (e.g., extending to 50 epochs) would likely yield further performance 
  improvements.
* No Overfitting: Training and validation loss curves remained closely 
  aligned, proving that the model generalizes well and the dropout/data 
  augmentation parameters are well-balanced.
